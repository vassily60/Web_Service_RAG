Welcome to the CVE Document Search Portal, powered by Generative AI

You are now in the hub of the portal, and can navigate to:

Search in the library: Ask a question in natural language to all the documents, receive an answer and the text extracts used to justify the answer. For exemple: Redige moi une clause de session

Search by document: Ask the same question in natural language to all the documents, receive an answer and the text extracts used to justify the answer for each of them. For exemple: Quels sont les signataires de ce documents

Saved Queries: Manage saved queries that can be shared with your team members and recall in the searchs

Documents Library: Manage the documents in the library by adding tags and metadatas

Metadata Management: Manage the metadata of the documents in the library

Synonyms Management: Manage the synonyms

Logout: Logout from the portal

For any questions, please contact the administrator.
